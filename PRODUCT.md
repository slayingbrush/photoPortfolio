# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Art directors, editorial photo editors, potential commercial clients, and people who already know what good photography looks like. They visit in a work context — they're deciding whether to hire, pitch, or feature. They have seen a thousand photographer portfolios and have a finely tuned reflex for anything that feels templated or safe.

## Product Purpose

A photographer's primary business card — the thing that wins or loses the job before a single email is sent. Success means a visitor lands, feels the photographer's specific sensibility immediately, and knows they need to reach out. Not "nice work," but "this person is the one."

## Brand Personality

Silk / obsidian / lacquer. Refined, surface-conscious, deliberate. The kind of restraint that reads as expensive, not cautious. Color is used like light catching a surface — precise, vivid, unexpected.

## Anti-references

- Squarespace/Format photographer templates — safe serif + clean grid + tasteful beige = forgettable
- 500px / Flickr gallery aesthetic — full-bleed dark bg, white Helvetica, every photographer's portfolio
- AI-generated editorial site — Cormorant Garamond + Space Grotesk + warm cream + eyebrow label on every section
- Fashion look-book white space — oversized margins, logo-scale type, zero information density

## Design Principles

1. **Surface over decoration.** Every color decision should feel like light reflecting off a material, not paint applied to a surface. The palette is vivid but controlled.
2. **Let the photographs do the work.** Typography and layout create the architecture the photos live inside — never compete with them.
3. **Deliberate marks.** Nothing incidental. If a color appears, it means something. If a line breaks a certain way, it means something. A portfolio that looks "AI-made" is a client lost.
4. **Range without chaos.** Documentary, portrait, landscape, street — the breadth is an argument for versatility, not a reason to scatter the visual voice.
5. **Expensive restraint.** Restraint is not absence of decision. Every empty space is a held breath, not an oversight.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast ratios especially critical given dark background — text must read at full luminosity. Reduced motion respected. All interactive elements keyboard-navigable.
